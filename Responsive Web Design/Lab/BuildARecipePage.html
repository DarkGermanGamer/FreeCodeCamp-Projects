Scrambled Eggs

A simple recipe for beginners.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 Small Onion
  • 1 Clove of garlic
  • 100g Diced Ham
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Other spices and herbs

Instructions

  1. Cut your garlic and onion into small cubes
  2. Fry the diced ham in a pan, then add the cut garlic and onion
  3. Crack your eggs open on the brim of a mug or small bowl. Make sure that no piece of the shell makes it into the mug/bowl
  4. Take a whisk and mix the egg white and the yolk
  5. Add your scrambled eggs to the pan and keep stirring it
  6. Add a pinch of salt and pepper
  7. If you like, you can add other spices or maybe some herbs
  8. Once your scrambled eggs are ready, serve them on a plate
